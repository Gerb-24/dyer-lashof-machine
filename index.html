Dyer-Lashof machine

A tool to compute the mod 2 homology of the free operad algebra of a spectrum

operad:

Use this textarea to write a module over the dual steenrod algebra.
Here the first line indicates the amount of generators,
the third line indicates in which degrees the generators live in,
and from the fifth line and onwards are meant to specify the action of the dual steenrod algebra.
( without having any line breaks, so line 6 is also meant to define the action )

For example the line, if we were to have |x_5| = 5 and |x_2|=|x_3|=2, then the line '5 3 2 3 1' would specify that Sq_3(x_5) consists of two summands, that being x_3 and x_1.
